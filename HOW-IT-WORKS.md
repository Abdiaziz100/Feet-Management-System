# 🚗 FLEET MANAGEMENT SYSTEM - DEMO GUIDE

## WHAT IS THIS SYSTEM?
Think of it like Uber's admin panel - it helps manage cars, drivers, and trips.

## 🎯 SIMPLE EXAMPLE: TAXI COMPANY

### SCENARIO: You own 3 taxis and need to manage them

---

## 📱 STEP 1: LOGIN
- Open: http://localhost:5173
- Username: `admin`
- Password: `password`
- Click "Sign In"

---

## 🚗 STEP 2: ADD YOUR CARS
**Go to "Vehicles" page**

Add these 3 cars:

2. Plate: `KCA 002B` - Status: Active  
3. Plate: `KCA 003C` - Status: Maintenance

## 👨‍💼 STEP 3: REGISTER YOUR DRIVERS
**Go to "Drivers" page**

## 💡 WHAT THIS SYSTEM HELPS YOU DO:

### FOR BUSINESS OWNERS:
- **Know where all cars are** (Live Tracking)
- **Track daily income** (Trip records)
- **Monitor expenses** (Fuel + Maintenance costs)
- **Manage driver performance** (Trip history per driver)
- **Handle emergencies** (Send alerts to drivers)

### FOR FLEET MANAGERS:
- **Assign cars efficiently** (Assignments page)
- **Schedule maintenance** (Maintenance tracking)
- **Monitor fuel efficiency** (Fuel reports)
- **Track vehicle utilization** (Reports page)

### REAL-WORLD BENEFITS:
- **Reduce costs** by tracking fuel and maintenance
- **Improve safety** with emergency alerts
- **Increase efficiency** by knowing vehicle locations
- **Better customer service** by tracking trip history
- **Business insights** from comprehensive reports
